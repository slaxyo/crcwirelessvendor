About the Market

The Clarkston Summer Market is a community-driven event bringing together families, friends, and neighbors to celebrate culture, diversity, and creativity in the heart of Clarkston.

What to Expect

Live Performances

Enjoy music, dance, and spoken word performances from local talent.

Food Trucks

Sample delicious food from a range of cultures and cuisines.

Games & Activities

Family-friendly fun including games, raffles, and interactive booths.
